about me

I am a second-year graduate student at National Tsing Hua University advised by Prof. Min Sun. I am interested in computer vision, machine learning, and data infrastructure. My current research focuses on human tracklet clustering based on temporal and spatial information. Before that, I worked on knowledge distillation for object detection.

Previously, I was an undergrad researcher at Vision Science Lab where I worked on object detection in aerial images. I initiated the largest curated list of tiny object detection academic resources on GitHub.

I had been doing some course projects during my studies and organizing a study group (suspended for now) in which we discussed technical interview preparation stuff online.

In my free time, I participate in online programming competitions. Here is my codeforces, atcoder and clist. I also occasionally maintain my templates, write up notes (in Chinese) and contribute solutions to USACO Guide.
